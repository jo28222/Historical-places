The Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is one of the Seven Wonders of the World, located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh. It was built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Qutub Minar

Qutub Minar is a UNESCO World Heritage Site located in Delhi. Standing at 73 meters, it is the tallest brick minaret in the world, built during the Delhi Sultanate.

Red Fort

The Red Fort in Delhi is an iconic symbol of India's history. It was the main residence of the emperors of the Mughal Dynasty for around 200 years.
